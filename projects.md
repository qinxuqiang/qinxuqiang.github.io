## Research Software

My research often involves developing specialized software for specific problems. While not all of this code is publicly available (some is still under active research), I strive to make tools available whenever possible.

### Areas of Focus

- **Deep Learning Frameworks**: Custom architectures and training pipelines
- **NLP Tools**: Text processing, tokenization, and language model utilities
- **Mathematical Software**: Computational algebra and geometry tools
- **Data Analysis**: Tools for analyzing and visualizing research data
- **Reproducibility**: Scripts and configs for reproducing research results
